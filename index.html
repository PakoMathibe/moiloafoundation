The Dr T Moiloa Foundation

The Foundation has been a lifelong struggle and journey of its founder to incorporate faith and transform those messages of hope into principles of human significance. The aim of this foundation is to gather enough resources through the help of those in positions to help the under privileged woman and children in and around the communities of Rustenburg.

Positive Impact on Children

The Foundation knows that the children we positively impact today will become the leaders of tomorrow in our communities.

Collaberation with other NGO'S

We work closely with non-profit Organizations and community programs within our region that are in alignment with our values.

Feeding and housing schemes

Feeding and housing schemes that maximize the living conditions of our people, helping with eradicating poverty and homelessness.

FUNDRAISING

Make a change, make a donation

Every Foundation relies on the funds they are able to raise through activities and programs that allow donations to the Organisation to be possible , these funds will be used in programs that go from housing all the way to food security.
